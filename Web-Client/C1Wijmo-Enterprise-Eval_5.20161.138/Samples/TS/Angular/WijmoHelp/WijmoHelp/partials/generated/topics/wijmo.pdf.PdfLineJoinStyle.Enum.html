Specifies the shape to be used at the corners of paths that are stroked.

Values

Name Description
Bevel The two segments are finished with butt caps and the resulting notch beyond the ends of the segments is filled with a triangle.
Miter The outer edges of the strokes for the two segments are extended until they meet at an angle.
Round An arc of a circle with a diameter equal to the line width is drawn around the point where the two segments meet.
